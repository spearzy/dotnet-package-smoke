# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful and welcoming place for contributors and users.

Participants are expected to communicate with patience, assume good intent where reasonable, and keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behaviour

Examples of positive behaviour include:

- using welcoming and inclusive language
- giving constructive feedback
- accepting responsibility and apologising when mistakes happen
- focusing on what is best for the project and its users
- showing empathy towards other community members

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or conduct
- deliberate intimidation
- publishing private information without permission
- sustained disruption of project discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behaviour they consider inappropriate, harmful, or disruptive.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, contact the project maintainer privately.

Reports will be reviewed and handled as respectfully and confidentially as reasonably possible.
